      Practical Tips

      Hydration & Sleep

      • 3–4L water daily, more on training days
      • 7–8 hrs sleep — recovery drives fat loss as much as training does

      What to Avoid

      • Fried snacks, sugary drinks, refined flour (maida)
      • Alcohol — limit strictly, especially outside the Sunday allowance

      A Note on the Deficit

      1200–1500 kcal combined with 5 tough sessions/week is an aggressive plan. Re-check your weight and energy weekly — if you're feeling drained, dizzy, or performance is tanking, it's worth adding 150–200 kcal back in or speaking with a doctor/nutritionist. This app is a planning tool, not medical advice.
