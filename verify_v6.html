      Practical Tips

      Hydration & Sleep

      • 3–4L water daily, more on training days
      • 7–8 hrs sleep — recovery drives fat loss as much as training does

      What to Avoid

      • Fried snacks, sugary drinks, refined flour (maida)
      • Alcohol — limit strictly, especially outside the Sunday allowance

      A Note on the Deficit

      1200–1500 kcal combined with 6 tough sessions/week is an aggressive plan. Re-check your weight and energy weekly — if you're feeling drained, dizzy, or performance is tanking, it's worth adding 150–200 kcal back in or speaking with a doctor/nutritionist. This app is a planning tool, not medical advice.

      How the engine works (honesty note)

      The calendar covers any date because it's computed live from your start date — not stored day-by-day — so it works the same on day 1 and 30 years from now. Exercise variety across repeated 6-week blocks comes from a deterministic rotation through the exercise repository, not hand-written content for literally every future week, so the same split can recur in a different form roughly every 30–40 weeks. Check-in photos older than your most recent 8 weeks are pruned automatically to keep the app fast and storage-light — the weight and date for those weeks are kept forever.
